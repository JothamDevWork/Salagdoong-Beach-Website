Tours – Guided & Self-Guided

Tour Packages

Choose the tour experience that fits your style and budget

🛵 Self-Guided Scooter Tour

  • Rent a scooter from Siquijor town
  • Approx. ₱350–₱500/day rental
  • Freedom to stop anywhere you like
  • Covers Salagdoong, Cambugahay Falls, Lazi Church
  • Tip: Rent from ferry terminal upon arrival

🛺 Tricycle Island Tour

  • Hire a tricycle with a local driver-guide
  • Approx. ₱800–₱1,200 per day
  • Great for groups or families
  • Driver knows the island well
  • Includes stops at popular beaches and falls

⛵ Island Hopping Tour

  • Boat tour around Siquijor's coastline
  • Approx. ₱1,500–₱2,500 per boat
  • Includes snorkeling at Paliton & Salagdoong
  • Stops at hidden coves and sandbars
  • Book through local resorts in San Juan

Sample Day Tour Itinerary (Southern Loop)

A recommended route that includes Salagdoong Beach

Time Stop
Morning Cambugahay Falls (rope swing)
Late Morning Lazi Church & Convent
Lunch Local eatery near Lazi
Early Afternoon Salagdoong Beach (cliff diving & swimming)
Late Afternoon Drive back through Salagdoong Forest
Evening Dinner at San Juan beach bar

💡 Tip: Arrive at Salagdoong in the early afternoon when tides are higher — this ensures the cliff diving platforms are safe to use.
