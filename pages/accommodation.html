Accommodation – Where to Stay

Accommodation Near Salagdoong Beach

From budget camping to luxury resorts — there's something for every traveler

🏕️ Budget Stays

Camping at the Beach

  • Tent rental available on-site
  • Approx. ₱200–₱300 per night
  • Fall asleep to the sound of the waves
  • Basic restrooms nearby

Kalinaw Inn ⭐ 4.9

  • Affordable rooms with personalized service
  • Ideal for solo travelers and backpackers
  • Friendly staff with local knowledge
  • Approx. ₱600–₱900/night

Destiny Rainbow Beach Resort

  • Budget camping near the shore
  • Simple fan rooms available
  • Beachfront location
  • Approx. ₱500–₱1,000/night

🏨 Mid-Range Stays

Suite Princess Beach Resort ⭐ 4.1

  • Mid-range villas with pool access
  • Sea-view rooms available
  • On-site restaurant and bar
  • Approx. ₱1,500–₱2,500/night

Hotel Agripino

  • Air-conditioned twin rooms
  • Towels and toiletries provided
  • Ask for a room with Maria Bay view
  • Approx. ₱800–₱1,200/night

Kalachuchi Beach Resort ⭐ 4.8

  • Privacy-focused garden villas
  • Lush tropical setting
  • Ideal for couples
  • Approx. ₱2,000–₱3,500/night

🌟 Luxury Stays

Coco Grove Beach Resort ⭐ 4.5

  • Top choice for luxury in Siquijor
  • Three pools and full dive facilities
  • Multiple dining venues
  • Wi-Fi and dive shop on-site
  • Approx. ₱4,500–₱8,000/night

U Story Resort ⭐ 4.7

  • Modern design with craft cocktail bar
  • Stunning sunset views from the deck
  • Boutique rooms with premium finishes
  • Island hopping tours arranged
  • Approx. ₱3,500–₱6,000/night

Accommodation Tips

📅 Book in Advance: Siquijor is a popular destination during peak season (December–May). Reserve your accommodation at least 2–4 weeks ahead, especially for beachfront resorts.

📍 Location Note: Most resorts are in San Juan (western side). Salagdoong Beach is on the eastern side (Maria). Consider staying in Maria or Lazi if you plan to spend most of your time at Salagdoong.
