Attractions – What to See & Do

Main Attractions at Salagdoong Beach

Explore what makes this beach Siquijor's most thrilling spot

Cliff Diving Platforms

  • 5 meters and 10 meters high
  • Safe only during high tide
  • Free (included in entrance fee)
  • Lifeguard on duty

Water Slide

  • Rock-carved slide twisting into the ocean
  • May not always be functional depending on maintenance
  • Great for families and beginners
  • Located on the small island rock

Snorkeling Spots

  • Colorful coral reefs just offshore
  • Tropical fish including clownfish ("Nemo")
  • Clear "jelly-colored" water for excellent visibility
  • Snorkel gear available for rent

Two Sandy Coves

  • First cove: ideal for snorkeling and swimming
  • Second cove: shallow, calm, great for families
  • White sand lined with coconut palms
  • Picnic tables and shaded areas available

Salagdoong Forest Reserve

  • Man-made molave forest surrounding the beach
  • Scenic shaded drive to the shore
  • Home to various bird species
  • Great for nature photography

Small Rock Island

  • Natural rock formation just off the shore
  • Houses the cliff diving platforms
  • Spectacular panoramic views of the sea
  • Walkable from the beach at low tide
