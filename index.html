About Salagdoong Beach


Salagdoong Beach is one of Siquijor's most famous coastal attractions. It is known for its thrilling 5-meter and 10-meter cliff diving platforms, crystal-clear turquoise water often called "jelly-colored," and two white-sand coves lined with coconut palms. The beach is located inside the Salagdoong Forest Reserve, a man-made molave forest that offers a scenic, shaded drive before you reach the shore.
